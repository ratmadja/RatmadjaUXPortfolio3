Furniture design

I have a fascination with off-the-shelf hardware pieces. I like how they can be assembled in such a way as to serve different purposes—like this series of floral vases made of copper pipes and glass. Each flower vase is designed to highlight the beauty of a single flower by elevating the flower head to be at eye-level when one sits on a table.
