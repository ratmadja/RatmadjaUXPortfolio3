WORK

Interaction Design

View Project

Ingrain is a meal-planning app from a plant-based perspective. I examined the granular details behind the use of motion to support usability. I then applied the motion design principles in creating high-fidelity interactive prototypes for 3 key sequences: onboarding, meal-planning, and meal-tracking.

iOS User Interface Design

View Project

This is a story of putting usability ahead of expressivity in designing the user interface of Ingrain, a meal-planning app for people who wish to transition to plant-based eating. I learned to be ruthless about editing and distilling screen layouts down to a level of simplicity that optimizes content legibility.

UX Strategy

View Project

Validating the riskiest product assumptions and discovering insights about the users' mental models—which resulted in two strategic pivots in defining the target audience of a meal-planning app. I learned to let go of my notions of what a product should be and let the data guide me to what it needs to be.
