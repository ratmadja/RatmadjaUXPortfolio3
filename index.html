WORK

Interaction Design for iOS

View Project

Ingrain is a plant-based, meal-planning app for people who wish to transition to plant-based eating and heal their bodies with wholesome food.

I examined the granular details behind the proper use of animation and applied motion design principles to create high-fidelity interactive prototypes of onboarding, browsing, and food log activities.

Mobile App Design for iOS

View Project

Putting usability ahead of expressivity in designing an app user interface of Ingrain, a plant-based, meal-planning app for people who wish to transition to plant-based eating to improve their health.

This is a story of putting usability ahead of expressivity—by ruthlessly editing my work to a level of simplicity that optimizes content legibility.

UX Strategy

View Project

Validating my assumptions of the user’s mental model with user surveys and interviews—and responding by making strategic pivots in defining the target audience of a meal-planning app.

I learned to let go of my notions of what this product should be—and let the data guide me to what it needs to be.
